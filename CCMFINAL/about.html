About Us

Get to know our people

Gail Storm, CEO and Co-Founder

Always on time, never too early or too late. Gail loves watches so much so that here childhood dream was be a wrist model for only the finest watch makers. Gail's sense of wrist fashion and morbid humor was in part the inspiration for the WatchamaWidget. Gail has overseen all business and marketing of the WatchamaWidget.

Adam Zapel, Product Designer & Co-Founder,
Regrets selling share in company so early

Adam was always late regardless of if he wore a watch or not. When he was late for his own graduation, Gail joked “maybe if your watch caused you physical pain when you were late, you would stop being late”, and wahlah, the idea of the WatchamaWidget was born. Adam designed and constructed the many prototypes.

Zapp Brannigan, Product Tester

Zapp was always the daredevil, willing to accept any bar bet and was often heard in the cafeteria demanding, “how much will you pay me if I eat that?”. When the human society refused out application to test on animals, Zapp stepped up! Without Zapp there would be no WatchamaWidget. Zap always wears our lightning bolt mascot custom to sales events
