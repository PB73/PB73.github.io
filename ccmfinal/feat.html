Features

Never miss a direction

Sync Google maps with your WatchamaWidget and you will be alerted of upcoming driving directions by means of small electric shocks to your Pronator Quadratus muscle.

Full autopilot mode

Simply plug the autopilot receiver into your cars diagnostic port, sync it with your WatchamaWidget, and allow our advanced shock system to control your arms motor reflexes to glide through steering while autopilot adjusts your speed.

No look reminders

Sync WatchamaWidget with your Google calendar, email, and social media sites to get customizable shock updates. You can set the duration, pattern, and intensity of the shock so you do not even need to look at the screen to know what you are being reminded of

Improve typing accuracy

Our software will automatically correct typos based off our muscle readings. As our software builds a profile of your typing errors, our shock system will positively reinforce correct typing with massaging zaps while continued typos will trigger insulting audio feedback.

DietSuccess app

Let WatchamaWidget control our arm movements to prevent unhealthy food related motions, such as reaching into bags of chips or spooning ice cream from the container. Our DietSuccess app automatically uploads your progress reports to Facebook and Twitter. Public accountability will further motivate successful dieting

The lap of luxury

With our velvet interior wrist band you will never notice the metal prods or electric shocks. After a hard day of repetitive arm work enjoy the relaxing massage app that stimulates your arm muscles releasing tension while simultaneously working them out.
